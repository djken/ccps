Part One

1 - Visiting the Hospital

1 - Visiting the Hospital

Yesterday, I visited my grandmother in the hospital. She is recovering from a fall. She slipped on some ice in the driveway. She has a broken hip and a lot of bruises. She had surgery on Friday. The doctor fixed her hip. He inserted some metal pins into the bone.

The pins hold the bone together. She has to stay in the hospital for a few weeks. She needs to stay in bed so her hip can heal. The hospital is boring. She doesn't have many things to do. I am going to visit her a few times every week. We can play cards or watch movies together.

2 - A Late Night

2 - A Late Night

I am so sleepy today. I stayed up too late last night. My grandmother is visiting for the weekend. We had a special party for her birthday. We invited our relatives.

My mom cooked her favorite food and baked a cake. We played games and looked at old pictures. My grandmother opened her gifts. She enjoyed the evening. Everyone stayed for a long time. My mom said to sleep early. I asked her if I could stay up.

She said I could stay up for another hour. After an hour, I tried to sleep. I closed my eyes, but I couldn't fall asleep. I stared at the clock. I finally fell asleep after midnight.

3 - Another Busy Day

3 - Another Busy Day

It's 8 o'clock and I am definitely ready for bed. I had such a busy day today. I had to wake up very early. I had tennis practice in the morning. I have a big tournament next weekend.

It is for the championship. Afterward, my mom and I went to a shopping center to buy a birthday gift for my best friend, Sally. I picked a sweater in her favorite color. Then, I went home to take a shower.

We had to leave for the birthday party after my shower. We went roller skating. I had so much fun. At the roller-skating rink, we ordered pizza and had cake and ice cream. After dinner, she opened her presents. She really liked my gift.

My dad picked me up at the rink. On the way home, my dad's car got a flat tire. He changed it with the spare tire in the trunk. We finally arrived home.

4 - Math Test Stress
4 - Math Test Stress

I am so stressed right now. I have a big math test this week. I am really worried about it. I think I am going to fail. I don't understand the concepts very well. Last night, I was so frustrated with myself. I did not know how to solve the problems.

I asked my parents for help. They were able to explain a few of the problems, but I was still having trouble with most of them. I don't know what I am doing wrong. My mom wants to hire a tutor.

My dad thinks that is a good idea. I'm going to go talk to my teacher tomorrow. I hope he can help me. I need to pass this test.

5 - A Group Project
5 - A Group Project

I am so annoyed with my friend Stephanie. She is so irresponsible. We are working together on a class project and she never helps. Each person was assigned a task for the project.

She didn't do her share. Yesterday, we had a group meeting at the library to complete our project. We agreed to meet after school. We all went to the library, but she wasn't there.

She didn't show up. We had to finish without her. I called her home. Her mom said she was at a friend's house. I saw her at school the next day.

She said she didn't know about the meeting but I know she wasn't telling the truth. We reminded her many times. I really don't want work with her anymore.

6 - An Accident
6 - An Accident

This morning, I had an accident while playing outside. I fell off my bicycle. Luckily, I had my helmet on. I hit my elbow on the ground and cut my hand. I also hurt my ankle.

My parents took me to the hospital. It wasn't serious. They washed off the blood and put a bandage on my cut. After we got home, my parents let me rest on the couch.

They gave me ice cream to make me feel better. I was worried my bicycle was broken. I love my bicycle. It was a birthday present from my grandparents. I want to ride it again. I hope my ankle gets better soon.

7 - A Day Outside
7 - A Day Outside

It was such a nice day, I stayed outside all day. In the morning, I went for a walk with my brother and our dog. Then, we came home and my mom made us lunch. We wanted to have a picnic in the backyard.

My mom made sandwiches. We laid out a blanket on the grass. After we finished our lunch, my brother and I threw away our trash. My mom came outside with us to work in the yard. There was a storm last weekend so it was still a mess.

The wind blew a lot of debris onto the lawn. I helped to rake the lawn and my brother mowed the grass. Many weeds grew in the garden this week. I pulled the weeds and my mom planted some new flowers. Then my dad came home after work. We decided to have a barbecue.

We cooked hamburgers and hot dogs on the grill and relaxed in the yard. I'm so glad the weather is nice again.

8 - A Bad Friend
8 - A Bad Friend

My friend, Jeff, is not a very nice person. Sometimes, he is rude. He wanted to study for a test together. So, we agreed to meet at his house. When I got there, I rang the doorbell. No one answered the door.

I called his house. No one answered the phone either. I waited for him outside. I began to worry that something was wrong. He finally showed up with his parents. He didn't even say he was sorry. He said he forgot.

I didn't believe him, but I forgave him anyway. His parents didn't even know I was coming. He didn't tell them. When we finally sat down to study, he turned on some loud music.

His mom told him to turn it down because it was too noisy. Instead, he started singing really loudly. I finally had enough and went home. When I get mad at him, he doesn't even care. I don't know if I want to be his friend anymore.

9 - A Really Good Day
9 - A Really Good Day

I had a really good day today. In the morning, I went for a bike ride with a friend. We rode down to the park and swam in the lake. Then we had a picnic lunch.

After lunch, we drove to the stadium to watch a professional soccer match. Our local team won so I was really happy. Then my mom cooked tacos for dinner. Tacos are my favorite. Plus, I finally found my library book. It was missing for two weeks.

My mom accidentally put it away in my sister's room. Only one bad thing happened. I fought with my sister. She borrowed a dress from me and spilled something on it. She is going to get it cleaned but I'm worried that it is ruined. Otherwise it was a perfect day.

10 - A Weekend with...
10 - A Weekend with my Grandparents

Last weekend, I visited my grandparents. They live in another city. On Friday, my parents drove me to their house. It is a long trip. Sometimes, I fall asleep. Other times, I bring a video to watch in the car.

When we arrived, my grandmother was cooking my favorite food, spaghetti and meatballs. On Saturday, we went to the park and I met a friendly dog. We also went to the petting zoo in the park and fed the animals. A goose bit my finger.

My finger bled, but I didn't cry. My grandmother put a bandage on it and it felt better. In the evening, we went out to dinner and saw a movie. On Sunday, we walked along the beach to collect seashells.

My grandfather said he knew an area that had a lot of shells. So, he led us there. I collected so many shells. I'm going save them in a big shoebox. I love spending time with my grandparents. I can't wait to go back.
